TLDR

Good

  • Clear and engaging writing style that makes complex concepts accessible.
  • Well-researched with plenty of real-life examples to illustrate points.
  • Provides actionable steps at the end of each chapter.

Bad

  • Some sections feel overly simplified, lacking depth for advanced readers.
  • The pacing slows down in the middle, with redundant explanations.
  • Few examples may seem outdated or irrelevant to current trends.

Takeaways

  • Consistent small actions lead to significant long-term changes.
  • Understanding the "why" behind behaviors is essential for lasting change.
  • Breaking goals into manageable steps increases the likelihood of success.
