Lebkuchen Kuchen

50 min

Easy

4

Ingredients

Dough

1kg

Swiss brown flour

1l

milk

500g

white sugar

500g

brown sugar

50g

gingerbread spice

8 Tbsp.

sunflower oil

4 Tbsp.

cocoapowder

2 pkg.

baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees conventional (or 160 degrees convection).
  2. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa, spice & baking powder and create a vulcano.
  3. Add milk slowly, mixing well, then add oil.
  4. Spread dough evenly on baking tray.
  5. Bake for ~30 min on the middle rack.
