Apple Crumble

1h 10min

Easy

4

Ingredients

Crumble

150g

butter (cold)

125g

flour

125g

hearty oat flakes

125g

sugar (50:50 brown/white)

1 pinch

salt

Filling

750g

apples

50g

sugar

2 tbsp.

lemon juice

1 tsp.

cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees conventional. Grease casserole dish.
  2. Peel apples and cut into slices/cubes. Mix with cinnamon, sugar and lemon juice.
  3. Mix crumble ingredients and knead.
  4. Add apples to casserole and distribute crumble evenly.
  5. Bake for ~45 min on middle rack.
